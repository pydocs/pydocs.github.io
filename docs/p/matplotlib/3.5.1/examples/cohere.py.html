Plotting the coherence of two signals

An example showing how to plot the coherence of two signals.

... 
... fig, axs = plt.subplots(2, 1)

... axs[0].set_xlim(0, 2)

... axs[0].set_ylabel('s1 and s2')
... axs[0].grid(True)
...
